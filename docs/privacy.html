Privacy Policy

TinniCalm

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: TinniCalm collects zero data. We have no servers, no analytics, no accounts, and no ads. Everything you do in the app stays entirely on your device.

1. Information We Collect

None. TinniCalm does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data whatsoever. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Usage data, analytics, or telemetry
  • Location data
  • Health or medical data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Crash reports or diagnostics

2. Data Storage

All app data — including your sound preferences, custom presets, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

TinniCalm does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-processing services. There are no SDKs, pixels, or external scripts embedded in the app.

4. Accounts & Authentication

TinniCalm does not require or offer user accounts. There is no sign-up, login, or authentication of any kind.

5. In-App Purchases

TinniCalm offers an optional one-time premium unlock for $9.99. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment information. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

6. Children's Privacy

Because TinniCalm collects no data from anyone, it inherently complies with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar laws. We do not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.

7. International Users

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfers. TinniCalm complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations by design — there is simply no data to protect on our end.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Given our commitment to zero data collection, we do not anticipate material changes. Should TinniCalm ever begin collecting data in the future, we will notify users through an app update and request explicit consent.

9. Contact Us

10. Health Disclaimer

TinniCalm is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. The sounds and features provided are for relaxation and general wellness purposes only. If you experience tinnitus or any hearing-related condition, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
